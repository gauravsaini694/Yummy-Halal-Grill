Welcome to Yummy Halal Grill

Where Flavor Meets Tradition

At Yummy Halal Grill, we bring you a fusion of authentic halal flavors and modern street food favorites. From sizzling platters over rice to gourmet pizzas, our dishes are crafted with fresh ingredients and bold spices. Located in the heart of Queens Village, we’re committed to quality, affordability, and fast service — because good food should always be within reach. Whether you're craving something classic or something spicy, we’ve got something to satisfy every palate.
